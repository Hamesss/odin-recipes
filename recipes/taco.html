Taco

A famous and meaty dish from Mexico.

The taco is an amalgimation of many different foods and flavours, coming together in a delicious mix. The taco comes in many shapes in sizes. The variations come in the meat type, shell type, and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Taco spice
  • Hard taco shell
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Jalapeno
  • Salsa
  • Cheese

Instructions

  • Cook The ground beef, taco spice, and 1/4 cup of water in a pan.
  • Cook the shell for 10 minutes at 300F.
  • Cut the tomato into small cubes.
  • Break the lettuce into small pieces.
  • Put the ground beef in the shell.
  • Add the cheese to the taco.
  • Add the salsa to the taco.
  • Add the tomato pieces to the taco.
  • Add the jalapeno to the taco.
