Hotdog

A meal consisting of a long piece of meat, a bun, and various toppings.

Like the hamburger, the hotdog is quite versatile. Many of the ingredients found on a hamburger are also commonplace on a hotdog. The hotdog is also equally delicious, an immaculate collision of different flavours.

Ingredients

  • Hotdog meat
  • Hotdog bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Buns
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the hotdog.
  2. Grate some cheese.
  3. Place the hotdog on the bun.
  4. Toast the buns slightly.
  5. add ketchup.
  6. add mayonnaise.
  7. Add pickles.
  8. Add mustard.
  9. Add onions.
  10. Add cheese.
