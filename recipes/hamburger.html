Hamburger

A delicious sandwich and a staple of American culture.

The hamburger is delicious. It consists of meat (Usually beef), a bun, and many other ingredients of one's choosing. The flavour combinations are basically endless with a hamburger and always leaves you wanting more. This hamburger recipe utilizes the finest selection of ingredients for max tastiness.
