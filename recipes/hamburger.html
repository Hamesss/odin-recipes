Hamburger

A delicious sandwich and a staple of American culture.

The hamburger is delicious. It consists of meat (Usually beef), a bun, and many other ingredients of one's choosing. The flavour combinations are basically endless with a hamburger and always leaves you wanting more. This hamburger recipe utilizes the finest selection of ingredients for max tastiness.

Ingredients

  • Beef Patty
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Buns
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the Patty.
  2. Slice the tomato.
  3. Cut two slices of cheese.
  4. Break the lettuce into medium-sized pieces.
  5. Toast the buns slightly.
  6. On the bottom bun, add ketchup.
  7. On the bottom bun, add mayonnaise.
  8. On top of the ketchup and mayonnaise, place a tomato slice.
  9. Place the cooked patty on top of the tomato slice.
  10. Place the cheese on top of the patty.
  11. Place 2 or 3 pieces of lettuce on the cheese.
  12. Add pickles on top.
  13. Add mustard on top.
  14. Place the top bun on top of everything else.
